About Us

Vision provides a suitable platform for students of MANIT to showcase their technical skills. It motivates them to research and develop work in any engineering related field and guides them about the opportunities in their respective fields. The objective of the society is to bridge the gap between industry and students studying in technology-related fields, while promoting and providing an intellectually stimulating university environment for the students.

It was established in the year 2006 by the students of MANIT with a view to expand their knowledge and make them aware about the new technological advancements. It conducts informative workshops to help students sharpen their skills and develop ideas by learning new things. It also gives them a platform to showcase their technical talent and pursue their interests. The events held by the society are exciting as well as enlightening. The students participate in these events with enthusiasm and look forward to attending these.

Since its inception, it has done commendable work and received widespread appreciation from the faculty members and the alumni of the college.
